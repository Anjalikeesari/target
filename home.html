Nature refers to the physical world, including life, landscapes, and natural phenomena, and the inherent character of the universe. It encompasses everything from the air and water we rely on to the plants, animals, and ecosystems that surround us. The term can also refer to the weekly multidisciplinary scientific journal, Nature, published by Springer Nature
